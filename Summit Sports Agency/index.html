Why do I need an agent?

It is important to have representation to assist you in all aspects of your career and to ensure you get the type of contract and fair market value that you are entitled to based on your athletic abilities

Which sports does Summit Sports Group represent players for?

We represent players in all professional sports. If there is a player who needs representation to acheive their maximum compensation, we are willing to represent them.

Does Summit Sports Group represent talents outside of the sports world?

We feel that we can best represent our clients if we are more specialized in our representation, so that our negotiation can be more fine tuned.

Wouldn't I be better represented by an agency who specializes in my sport?

We employ agents who specialize in your sport to maximize your representation.

What qualifications do your agents have?

Our agents are certified by the players associations of whatever sports they work in.
